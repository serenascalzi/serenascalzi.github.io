About

As a graduate assistant in the Office of Online Education at UNLV, I assist teams of instructional designers and technologists in developing courses, resources, and assessments to support faculty and students in remote learning. As a graduate student in Educational Technology at UNLV, my research focuses on the intersection of education, technology, and creativity.

I earned my B.S. in Marketing and International Business from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University, along with certificates in Full Stack Engineering from PunchCode and Computer Graphics Design from Otis College of Art and Design. My professional experience includes design, technology, and marketing roles in both the private and the public sector, with over three years of experience in the higher education industry.
